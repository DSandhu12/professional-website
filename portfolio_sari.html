SARI is a powerful web-based Point of Sale and Inventory Management System specifically designed for micro, small, and medium enterprises (MSMEs), particularly sari-sari stores in the Philippines. The platform streamlines sales transactions, stock management, and financial record-keeping with an intuitive interface that empowers small business owners to take control of their operations.

Built with robust validation, sanitation, and database operations, SARI provides enterprise-level features in a package accessible to small retailers. The system includes role-based access control, real-time inventory tracking, comprehensive reporting, and audit logging‚Äîall critical features for modern retail management.
